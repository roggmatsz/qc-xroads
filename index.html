Queen City Crossroads

The Charlotte History Podcast

About The Podcast

Queen City Crossroads is about Americans at the crossroads of history and the choices they make when they get there.

We share real peoples’ explicit words as they navigate major historical events — like emancipation, world wars, school integration — and explore how they unfolded in one place: Charlotte, North Carolina.

Through this work, our goal is to inspire our listeners to make courageous choices today that benefit the common good.

The Problem

Many people in Charlotte believe:

  • Charlotte has no history.
  • No one in Charlotte is from Charlotte.
  • For one group to win, another has to lose.

In schools, history is often taught:

  • With a nationwide scope, without attention to what happened right here in our city.
  • With historically marginalized people’s voices missing or as isolated sidebars.
  • Without explicit connections to the present.

The Solution

Queen City Crossroads is a podcast designed to inspire the hearts and minds of a broad audience.

Each episode:

  • Puts explicit words from Charlotte's history into conversation with contemporary Charlotteans today.
  • Includes diverse voices by design to ensure a deeper, more complete understanding of our history.
  • Challenges the idea that one group has to lose for another to win. Instead, it's about joining forces -- no matter your background -- to create a city where we all thrive.
